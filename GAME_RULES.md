## 3. How to Win (Paylines)

A "Payline" is a specific pattern that lines cross the screen. You only win if symbols line up exactly on these paths.

*   **Rule 1: Left-to-Right**: All wins must start from Reel 1 (the far left). A combo like `Blank - A - A - A - A` pays nothing because it didn't start at the beginning.
*   **Rule 2: Consecutive**: Symbols must be next to each other on the line. `A - A - Blank - A` breaks the chain at 2 matches.
*   **Rule 3: 20 Lines**: There are 20 different patterns active on every spin.
    *   *Straight Lines*: Top row, Middle row, Bottom row.
    *   *V-Shapes*: Top-Left to Bottom-Middle to Top-Right.
    *   *Zig-Zags*: Various complex paths across the grid.

## 8. Common Myths & Mechanics

### "Is this a Card Game?"
**No.** While we use card symbols (A, K, Q, J, 10), this is **not** Poker or Blackjack.
*   **Card Games**: Use a deck of 52 cards. If you hold an Ace, there are only 3 Aces left in the deck.
*   **Slots**: Use **Reels** (spinning wheels). Each reel is independent and contains many copies of the same symbol. It is perfectly possible (and lucky!) to see **15 Aces** on the screen at the same time.

### "Is it due for a win?"
**No.** Every spin is calculated by a Random Number Generator (RNG) the instant you click the button. The machine has no memory. If you just hit a Jackpot, your odds of hitting another Jackpot on the very next spin are exactly the same.
